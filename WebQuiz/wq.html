For the system of linear equations x − y = 7 y = −x + 5 which of the following moves are correct?
(a)
− y = −x + 7 y = −x + 5
  (b)
− y = x + 7 y = −x + 5
(c)
y = x + 7 y = −x + 5
  (d)
y = −x − 7 y = −x + 5

Choice (a) is Correct!
If Claire is 1 then Betty is 2 and Alice is 4!
Choice (b) is Incorrect.
If Claire is 2 then Betty is 3 and Alice is 6.
Choice (c) is Incorrect.
If Claire is 3 then Betty is 4 and Alice is 8.
Choice (d) is Incorrect.
If Claire is 4 then Betty is 5 and Alice is 10.
