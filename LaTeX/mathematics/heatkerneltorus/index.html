2 Heat kernel on 𝕋n

d⁢mn⁢(x)=∏k=1nd⁢m⁢(xk)=∏k=1n(2⁢π)-1⁢d⁢xk=(2⁢π)-n⁢d⁢x,

4 Convergence of infinite products

If ck≥0, then for any n,

1+∑k=1nck≤∏k=1n(1+ck)≤exp⁡(∑k=1nck).

Thus, the limit of ∏k=1n(1+ck) as n→∞ exists if and only if

∑k=1∞ck<∞.

For the second inequality in Theorem 8, the limit of ∏k=1n(1+2⁢e-ak⁢t) as n→∞ exists if and only if

∑k=1∞2⁢e-ak⁢t<∞.
