For q≥1 let

𝒜q={aq:0≤a≤q,gcd⁡(a,q)=1}.

The sets 𝒜q are pairwise disjoint. In particular 0∈𝒜1 and 0∉𝒜q for q>1. We have

[0,1]∩ℚ=⋃q≥1𝒜q.

Write μ for Lebesgue measure on [0,1].

Theorem 1 (Khinchin).
