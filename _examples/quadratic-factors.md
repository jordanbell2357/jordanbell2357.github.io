$$
\begin{align*}
(qx+r)(sx+t)&=(qx)(sx)+(qx)(t)+(r)(sx)+(r)(t)\\
&=qsx^2+qtx+rsx+rt\\
&=(qs)x^2+(qt+sr)x+rt
\end{align*}
$$

$$ax^2+bx+c=(qx+r)(sx+t)$$

if and only if

$$
\begin{align*}
a&=qs\\
b&=qt+sr\\
c&=rt
\end{align*}
$$

$$
m = qt
$$

and

$$
n = sr
$$
