$$g(x)=ax^2$$.

Let $$A=(x_A,y_A)$$ be a point on the parabola. Then $$y_A=ax_A^2$$.

$$y-y_A=m(x-x_A)$$

$$g_m(x)=mx-mx_A+y_A$$

$$
\begin{gather*}
g(x)-g_m(x)=ax^2-(mx-mx_A+y_A)\\
g(x)-g_m(x)=ax^2-mx+mx_A-y_A\\
g(x)-g_m(x)=ax^2+b_mx+c_m\\
b_m=-m, \qquad c_m=mx_A-y_A
\end{gather*}
$$
