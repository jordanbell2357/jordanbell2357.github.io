$$p(x)=a(x-h)^2+k$$ and $$q(x)=mx+b$$.

Let $$r(x)=p(x)-q(x)$$.

$$
\begin{align*}
r(x)&=a(x-h)^2+k-(mx+b)\\
&=a(x^2-2hx+h^2)+k-mx-b\\
&=ax^2-2ahx+ah^2+k-mx-b\\
&=Ax^2+Bx+C
\end{align*}

for $$A=a$$, $$B=-2ah-m$$, and $$C=ah^2+k-b$$.
